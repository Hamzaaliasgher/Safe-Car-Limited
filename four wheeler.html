Four Wheeler Insurance



The coverage offered in liability policy differs from the comprehensive policy. While liability plan only covers third-party liability, the comprehensive one covers you for the damages to the vehicle and the owner which is called as Personal Accident Cover.

There are Generally Two Types of Four-wheeler Insurance Policies:


1. Collision insurance may cover damage to your car after an accident involving another vehicle and may help to repair or replace a covered vehicle.

2.Comprehensive insurance can provide an extra level of coverage in the instance of an accident involving another vehicle.
